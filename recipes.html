Bread & Butter Pickles

Ingredients: Cucumber, 1 shallot onion, 1 garlic clove, 100ml apple cider vinegar, 60ml water, 30ml caster sugar, 1 tbsp salt, 1 tsp mustard seeds, 1tsp chilli flakes, 1 star anise, 1 tsp black peppercorns, 1 tsp coriander seeds, 1 tsp ground cinnamon, 1 tsp tumeric

Instructions:

1. Slice cucumber and sprinkle with the salt and let sit over night, the next day drain excess liquid.

2. Mix water, vinegar, sugar and bring to boil then add spices. Leave to cool.

3. Once cooled, pour over sliced cucumber and jar.

Pretzels

Ingredients: 275ml warm water, 2.5tsp dried yeast, 120g baking soda, 1tsp salt, 1 tbsp brown sugar, 1/2 tbsp black treacle, 500g all-purpose flour, 2.1L water, Coarse sea salt for sprinkling

Instructions:

Preheat oven to 190c (fan)

1. Mix yeast to warm water and leave for one minute.

2. Mix together flour, treacle, brown sugar, salt and yeast / water solution.

3. Knead dough for three - five minutes and shape into a ball. Leave to rest in a bowl for ten minutes, covered with a wet towel.

4. Cut and shape dough.

5. Bring baking soda and water to a boil.

6. Stretch then drop pretzel into boiling water and leave for 20 - 30 seconds.

7. Remove pretzel. Drain off water and sprinkle on coarse sea salt.

8. Bake for 12 - 15 minutes, turning tray half way.

9. Remove from oven and allow to cool. Serve immediately, or freeze.

House Cake

Ingredients: 250g plain flour, 200g caster sugar, 150ml ev olive oil, 150g natural yoghurt, 3 tsp baking powder, 3 eggs, 1 lemon zest, 1 orange zest, 1/2 tsp vanilla essence, icing sugar for dusting

Instructions:

1. Set oven to 180c
2. Grease 23cm circular tin with neutral oil
3. Mix together dry ingredients in mixing bowl
4. Combine remaining ingredients into mixture and mix
5. Pour batter into tin
6. Bake for 35 minutes
7. Dust with plenty of icing sugar
8. Allow to cool for 10 minutes

Source: Lazy baking, Jessica Elliott Dennison. Recipe adjusted to reduce sugar.

House Granola

Ingredients: 120g rolled oats, 100g nuts (mix of your choosing, flaked almonds and pecans are our preferred), 100ml maple syrup or soft brown sugar, 80g seeds (mix of your choosing, sunflower and pumpkin are our preferred), 80g desiccated coconut, 50ml olive oil (good quality)

Instructions:

1. Set oven to 150c
2. Mix all dry ingredients (a part from coconut which will be toasted in a pan and added at the end.
3. Mix all wet ingredients
4. Combine dry and wet
5. Bake at 150c for 30 minutes (till golden brown)
6. Leave to cool, this will help everything get that extra crunch
7. Add coconut

Source: Cafe Cecilia, adapted to reduce sweetness and less specific on ingredients
